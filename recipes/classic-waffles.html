Classic Waffles

Description

Waffles are a classic breakfast dish to eat in the morning. This
traditional recipe will give it that classic golden brown-sugar
texture and nostolgic aroma. The best part is . . . it's very easy to make!
All it takes is adding the correct measurements and a bit of patience.
Now moving on to how much time it takes to make these delicious golden squares.
The prep time will take around 10 minutes, while cooking the whole batch
will take around 15 minutes. This batch will yield about 10 to 12 classic waffles.
It will total to 5 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Grab a large bowl for mixing. Mix the flour, salt, baking powder and sugar together. Preheat waffle iron to desired tempature.

  2. Grab a second bowl, beat the eggs. Then stir in the milk; butter and vanilla. Pour the liquid mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.

  3. Ladle the batter into preheated waffle iron. Cook until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
